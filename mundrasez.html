Mundra SEZ

Mundra SEZ (Special Economic Zone) is located in mundra district of kutch gujarat. the mundra Special Economic Zone was incorporated in 2003. it became India's first multi-product port-based special economic zone. Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Limited is the largest commercial ports operator in India accounting for nearly one-fourth of the cargo movement in the country. its presence across 10 domestic ports in six maritime states of gujarat.

Ambaji Warehouse Park is Fast growing extra-ordinary location just 3.6 kms. from Adani Port & SEZ and we provide all kind of warehousing solutions.

Why Choose Us

All Equipment

We have all types of equipment for moving material around a warehouse involves lifts and trucks of many kinds, such as pallet jacks, lift trucks, hand trucks, scissor lifts, stackers, etc

Labour Facility

Transportation
